How Do I Make My Purchases?

Youbuyweshipusa.com is a derivative of housakicks. With Us, you can buy any products from Maryland local shops and US based online retailers who do not ship locally or internationally. There are two ways to use our services, You buy directly and use our address or We buy on your behalf and ship the items to your address overseas!

Method I: You Buy

  1. Order item(s) online and have it shipped to our office. Items should be addressed to:
    • Your Name
    • 9401 Dunraven street
    • frederick, MD 2170
  2. Kindly email the following to "mistourasal@gmail.com":
    • Your delivery address and phone number
    • Purchase invoice showing item description and price.
  3. Our fees:
    • (10%) x Item cost
    • shipping cost to your destination.

Method II: We Buy on your behalf

There are two types of payments in the second method of purchasing

  1. FIRST PAYMENT:
    • ITEM PRICE
    • +SALES TAX
    • +SERVICE FEE (15%)
    • +DOMESTIC SHIPPING
  2. SECOND PAYMENT:
    • The second payment mainly covers shipping cost to your destination.

Package Consolidation/SHIPPING

  • For shipping to your destination, we use USPS by default because it is by far the cheapest shipping method. But we will gladly use another reliable shipping service of your choice. Our package consolidation gives you the opportunity to group multiple packages together and ship them in one package in order to save money.

HOW LONG DO YOU HOLD A PACKAGE FOR?

  • We will hold your package for only 14 days for free and for every additional day there will be a $1 charge.

WHAT FORMS OF PAYMENT DO YOU ACCEPT?

  • we accept paypal and all major credit/debit cards
